About Us

Who We Are

Building Nigeria's digital workforce, one skill at a time.

Our Story

Built for Every Nigerian

ABTECH Computer Academy was founded with one clear vision: to make digital skills accessible to every Nigerian regardless of age, background or experience level.

From school leavers looking for an edge, to traders wanting to market online, to teachers upgrading their skills — we serve them all with practical, affordable training that delivers real results.

Our Mission

Empowering People to Learn, Earn and Stay Relevant Through Digital Skills.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's most impactful digital skills training institution by 2030.
